About AuthentiHire

AuthentiHire is an AI-powered recruitment platform dedicated to ensuring fairness, transparency, and authenticity in digital hiring. Our system intelligently detects deepfakes, impersonation, and unethical behavior during video interviews.

We combine advanced machine learning, facial recognition, and behavioral analytics to make hiring decisions more reliable and free from fraud.
